def column_rename_multiple_same(file,col_name):
    cols = []
    count = 1
    for column in file.columns:
        if column == col_name:
            cols.append(col_name + f'{count}')
            count+=1
            continue
        cols.append(column)
    
    file.columns = cols

Conclusions¶

While Drama movies are the most produced, they do not hold the most popoular spot or even close to it and did not make the list of top profitable genres. The data studied here does not suggest that the most produced genre or popularity with a particular genre leads to increased revenue, there is no correlation.

Limitation¶

There are several limitations to this study, the biggest being the group polled. How large of a population was polled? What was the age bracket polled? Had everyone polled seen the same movies? Was each movie rated by the same amount of people? Did most people favor action over drama, etc? All of these factors may have influence on the outcome of the analysis. Other limitations could be the movies or lack of movies on the list. The language the movie is seen in could change perception. Or the fact that I took only the first genre when multiple were listed. All of these issues could have an impact on the analysis.
